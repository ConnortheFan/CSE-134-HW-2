Teaching you how to algorithmically solve any sudoku

Even though I am a bit of a Sudoku addict (especially with variants), I still sometimes struggle to see just the one move that will let colapse the puzzle. To fix this, and also to teach my friends how to do Sudoku as well, I made Sudoku Solver!
